About Us

We at Group Mafricom/ Mafcom are committed to providing people with essential goods and products, including: top quality used clothing and a variety of international, highly nutritious dairy products.

In 1991, Group Mafricom/Mafcom began as a trading company, importing quality and used clothing from Europe and America. From the very start, Group Mafricomwas dedicated to supporting local communities and economies-an ideal that we still uphold today. Our first customers were generally women who bought bales of clothing, which they would then sell on individually in the market. This did not only help generate an income for these women, but also gave people access to top quality, world-class clothing.

Since then, our company grew and expanded significantly.In 2001, we started importing milk powder from Ireland, selling tins, sacks and sachets of milk power to the mass market in Africa. By providing the public with wholesome milk, at an affordable rate, we were able to fulfil our goal of providing people with a top quality product that was both healthy and nutritious.

In 2005, Group Mafricom/Mafcom expanded into Angola, where we began selling our used clothing and our milk powder and. Four years later,

in 2009, our centres in the Republic of Congo opened selling the same products.

Thanks to the success and growth of Group Mafricom/Mafcom, after a few years, we decided to venture further into the food market and create our very own drinking yogurt-Energie. We entered the market by packing Energie in both bottles and pouches, so that it could be accessible to both the formal and informal markets. In January of 2012,we launched our solid yoghurt, also under the new Energie brand name. This new product, which is targeted towards children, helps provide them with the essential nutrients they need while growing up. With our extensive distribution network and the quality of all our products, we are proud to say that we are one of the leaders of the markets in these countries. Of course, in addition to offering consumers our high quality products, we at Group Mafricom/ Mafcom also support local groups, such as the Youth Football Association. They help sponsor young football clubs, by providing them with uniforms and food before matches and we're very proud to be associated with them. Secondly, we are supporters of sustainable development-our products are always bought at wholesale price and then sold individually-helping to provide jobs and ensuring the distribution of quality products. We will continue to be doing our part in helping to build stronger and healthier generations, all towards a better and brighter future. Help us help you!

Today Group Mafricom /Mafcom Group is present in 3 countries, the Democratic Republic of Congo, Republic of Congo and Angola with 5 factories and 16 operating centres in strategic locations.

Group Mafricom/Mafcom Group is member of local Business chambers in all three countries and is committed to good relationships with its workers, its customers, with the Governments and with all its trading partners
